About

General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University offers both undergraduate and postgraduate programs for military officer cadets and civilian students. The university currently comprises two faculties: the Faculty of Computing and the Faculty of Spatial Sciences. Each faculty encompasses several academic departments. The university's administration and academic operations are supported by both military personnel and civilian staff, including highly qualified lecturers.
